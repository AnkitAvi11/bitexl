Welcome Bitians..!!

Every day we work hand in hand with parents, teachers, educators and community members to facilitate students of BITL with ideas, thoughts, solutions, confessions, connections and realizations of what they are and should connect to. BITEXL showcase your abilities and requirements with your responsibilities. Our new site reflects this cooperative relationship and the role that so many Bitians play in ensuring student success.This site is designed to fulfill your requirements related to education in BIT. It provides you with services of open library and events of your college. Apart from education, the kit offers you a platform to showcase your talents by Blog writings, photography, videos and much more.
To make is site exciting Team BITEXL enables you to write confessions too. Aside from the site’s updated look, you will notice information is more easily accessible and organized with BIT students, parents and educators in mind. This site reflects our continued efforts to communicate effectively and transparently about education in BIT LALPUR.

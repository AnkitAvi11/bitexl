BIZWIT

The BIZWIT Society has been functioning in Birla Institute of Technology , Extension Lalpur for around 7 years now. The BIZWIT society is formed in a bid to introduce and promote events while enjoying the best of what college life has to offer. BIZWIT is run by the students for the students with an aim to unite each other. It has been an integral part of our college experience , offering the chance to explore new activities , find people with like minded interests and potentially enhance a student . The motto of BIZWIT Society is “WISDOM”, which concludes the quality of having experience , knowledge , and a good judgement in a student. BIZWIT Society provide student with the opportunity to try something you might never have thought of before. BIZWIT organize both online and offline events ,and provides prizes like i-phones and DSLR camera to the winners. It collects funds for prizes in form of sponsorship. BIZWIT is active in organizing range of events throughout the year . It has been providing students with beyond the classroom and campus learning opportunities through organizing various events.

DEBSOC

The Debating Society has and always be an intrinsic part of Birla Institute of Technology , Extension Lalpur . DEBSOC at BIT was started primarily to bring students having passion for debating together with those who seek benefits from enhanced debating skills. DEBSOC was established in the year 2002 ,by Dr . S Avis Haider and Prof. Amitabh Chandan . DEBSOC has a range of activities lined up throughout the year. “DEBSOC-break out of your shell” as the name spells aims to build self confidence and to be more comfortable and friendly with people. The DEBSOC acts as a network-lifeline for the students commune . It gives the chance to gain invaluable leadership and life skills . DEBSOC involves and engages students from organizing several events that aim at a holistic development of one’s skill . DEBSOC spreads consciousness about different social and education issues among the students with a view to make them worthy citizens. DEBSOC aims to develop critical analysis , persuasive skills , and public speaking in an informal environment.
